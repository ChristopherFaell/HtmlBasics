What is a Life Project?

A project life is a way to define and put in order what you'replanning to do with your time in different aspects.

Which aspects of your life you can organized by making a LP?

By making a Life Project you can define aspects like, your professional carrer, favorite sports, educational line, habits, hobbies and so on.

A LP could make you focused on what you really want to achieve in life...
